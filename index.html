Built on Trust. Defined by Excellence.

For over 50 years, Hira Sons has stood for integrity, craftsmanship, and enduring relationships. Our legacy is not just measured in years, but in the deep relationships we've nurtured with clients, partners, and communities across the globe.

Rooted in the Sanskrit word hīraka—meaning "diamond", our name translates to "Sons of Diamonds" — a reflection of our lineage, passion, and devotion to the brilliance and timeless value of this extraordinary gemstone and devotion to the trade.

For over half a century, we have delivered exceptional jewelry while upholding the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and responsibility. Our commitment to a responsible supply chain continues to shape the future of the industry and bring lasting value to everyone we serve.

Our offices are in Mumbai, India and Los Angeles, USA. You can reach out to us at janam@hirasons.in

We take our responsibilities seriously

Our commitment to responsible jewelry practices includes ethical conduct, fair treatment of individuals, and environmental stewardship. We prioritize product integrity, supply chain transparency, and stakeholder engagement to uphold industry standards and promote sustainability. Join us in our journey towards a responsible and ethical jewelry industry.
